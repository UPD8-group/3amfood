Why This Exists

Built for the 3AM hunger.

You've finished a shift. It's late. Google Maps is a maze. Every venue says "open" but half of them aren't. You just want food, now, sorted by what's still serving.

That's all 3AMFood does. No restaurant partnerships, no paid placements, no sponsored results. Just real data, real hours, sorted for the time you're actually awake.

Built by a Canberra shift worker who got sick of arriving at closed kebab shops at 2:47am.
